## 💡 The Strategy

> I have something most fresh data candidates don't: **real corporate experience + ML project background**.  
> My plant disease detection model = my first data pipeline project. I just didn't know it yet.

The path:
1. **Don't quit the day job** — stability = clear thinking
2. **SQL is the foundation** — master it before anything else
3. **Build in public** — every project goes on GitHub, every lesson on LinkedIn
4. **Specialist > Generalist** — decide DE or DA by Month 9, go deep
5. **Apply before feeling ready** — interviews teach faster than any course

---

## 🔥 The 8 Iron Rules

1. Never miss 2 days of learning in a row
2. Every project on GitHub — no exceptions
3. Don't quit the job until offer letter is signed
4. Projects > certificates (always)
5. Network before you need it
6. Monthly review — what did I build, learn, apply?
7. Keep the gym — tired brains learn 40% slower
8. Apply at Month 8, not Month 12
